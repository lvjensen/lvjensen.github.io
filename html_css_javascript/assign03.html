My name is Logan Jensen and I am a Data Science Major. I'm very passionate about exploring data and I really enjoy what I study. Education has always been important to me and I love going to school.
I was born and raised in Mesa, Arizona. Therefore, I am not a fan of the cold at all. I hope to someday move back South.
In my spare time, you might find me playing any sport or watching one of my favorite teams(Arizona Diamondbacks or Los Angeles Rams). I also really enjoy doing puzzles and playing games with my wife, who also just happens to be the most beautiful girl in the world.

As a Data Science major I've had great opportunities to excel and improve myself, as well as learn how to learn. I have had a lot of experience with statistical modeling, machine learning, data wrangling and visualization, computer programming, database design, and many other interesting concepts.
